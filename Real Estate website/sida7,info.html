My real estate business works like this.

I do not own any of the listed building. I do not sell houses, I sell the contract to the house with a reduced value. For example if a house is marketed at $500.000 and I successfully get a contract for $400.000 I will list it at $425.000 on my website. For every $100.000 I get of the listed value I make $25.000. Most houses I look for are a little broken down so that real estate investors can use it as a fix up and resell for more. I am just a middle man. If you aren't a real estate investor and still interested in a house I will still sell you the contract for $25.000 per $100.000 I reduced from market value. If I only lower the price by lets say $50.000 the cost of the contact will be reduced to $12.500. There is a contract you will have to sign when buying a contact. It says that if you buy the contract and then don't decide to buy the house I am not going to give you all you money back. I might buy the contract back for half price so make sure you really want the house you buy I am also not eligible for any damages to the house or if the value of the property drops. If you miss more then 3 payments on time the rent for the rest of the time left will jump to 8% $2250 per month + the month you missed

This is a contract you will have to sign before any business if you don't like it take your business els where thanks. Im looking forward to doing business with you.
